What is DanK Pay?

A seamless Web3 payment platform for cannabis retail, powered by Solana. Customers use $DKBUD to pay, while dispensaries receive USD without hassle.

Why it Matters

Legal cannabis remains cash-heavy. DanK Pay helps retailers get paid easily, and customers skip ATM fees. It’s crypto without the complexity.
